In the Dark Ages, the Kingdoms of England and Scotland emerged, with bloodlines of kings forming the first royal houses. The later royal houses would trace their ancestry to these initial royal houses. And when the King of Scotland inherited the Crown of England, he also inherited the Crown of Ireland.
