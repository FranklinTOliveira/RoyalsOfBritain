As warlordism fragmented the Roman Empire, the Roman splinter states on Great Britain were left exposed, as they turned to mercenaries to provide strength to their armies. The set of treacherous mercenaries that would take over would be called the Anglo-Saxons after the Angles and the Saxons that were the predominant tribal elements among them.

As it turned out, England, the (new) Land of the Angles, would be united by the Kings of Wessex, the West Saxon Land. This House of Wessex would form the royal family of England until its last king died childless. The succession crisis would bring the Kingdom of England under the House of Normandy.
